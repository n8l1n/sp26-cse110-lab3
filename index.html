Tetris Sailboat Opener

Overview

The Sailboat Opener is an opener in Tetris that sets up a T-Spin Single in the first bag, a T-Spin single in the second bag, and a T-Spin Triple in the third bag. Beyond this, there are multiple, viable continuations.

What to see from the video

  • It was able to effectively counter the opponent's Stickspin opener, which is a very common opener you will face in competitive Tetris.
  • After the opener, the board was ready to receive attacks and I was able to handle early attacks effectively.
  • I used a T-Spin double as my continuation which cleaned up my board even more, preparing me to receive attacks.
  • As a result of having three T-Spins from the start, I was quickly able to build up a Back-to-Back bonus (A feature in Tetr.io)

Today, we will be discussing the possibilities of the Sailboat opener!

Continuations

On the topic of continuations, there are multiple viable continuations after executing the sailboat opener. I would recommend practicing multiple different routes and playing around with the possibilities! Remember different continuations work for different situations...


For example, while the opener has a 77.38% chance of resulting in a perfect clear, this is not necessarily the best continuation from my experience since by the third bag, it is highly likely that the opponent has already sent at least 1 line making it impossible to fully clear the board. A color clear is not necessarily a bad thing, since that means your board is clean, and it will not be too difficult to access garbage sent by your opponent. Most of the time though, especially in higher level play, going for the perfect clear will break your back-to-back letting the opponent gain an advantage.

Final Thoughts

Overall, the Sailboat Opener is a very strong opener that has given me a lot of success in my competitive games. It may seem daunting at first, but with practice, it will come naturally.
